Can I float through Canoe Pass?




canoepass.info predicts whether the water is currently high enough to float through Canoe Pass (also known as Bargain Narrows) between Pender Harbour and Bargain Harbour, on the Sunshine Coast of British Columbia, Canada.


Canoes and kayaks can float through Canoe Pass when the tide at the pass is above about 10 feet; the rest of the time the pass is dry.

This site was created by William Grover and uses data from the Canadian Hydrographic Service's tide API to predict the tide height at Canoe Pass. The times are based on the predicted tides for Irvine's Landing with a 30-minute delay added to compensate for the distance from Irvine's Landing to Canoe Pass. The source code for this site is available on the site's GitHub repository.
